Dynamic Health Profiling Matrix

Our innovative approach classifies users into 4 distinct profiles based on Health Confidence and Clinical Health Status to deliver hyper-personalized care.

Four Distinct Health Profiles

We plot each user on a matrix with two axes:

  • Health Engagement Confidence: From Low Confidence to High Confidence
  • Clinical Health Status: From Low Health to High Health

This creates 4 unique profiles that allow our AI to understand not just what your health status is, but how you're equipped to manage it.

Living Healthy

High Health, High Confidence - Individuals who are in good health and actively engaged in maintaining their wellbeing.

Trotting Along

Low Health, Low Confidence - Individuals facing health challenges with limited engagement in their care.

Living Cautiously

High Health, Low Confidence - Individuals in good health but with concerns about maintaining their wellbeing.

Living Precariously

Low Health, High Confidence - Individuals facing health challenges but actively engaged in managing their condition.
